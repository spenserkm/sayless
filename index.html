Features

Two skills. One clean inbox.

Say Less bundles inbox cleanup and receipt scanning into Claude skills that work through natural conversation.

🗑

Inbox Cleanup

Scans your subscriptions, groups them by category, and lets you nuke entire categories with a single message. "Kill all the marketing stuff" actually works.

🧾

Receipt Scanner

Finds purchase receipts and order confirmations in Gmail, extracts structured data, and exports a clean CSV with totals by category.

💬

Conversational

No settings pages. No toggles. Just type what you want in plain English. "Unsubscribe from Groupon" or "show me what I spent on DoorDash."

🔒

Safe by Default

Always confirms before deleting. Protects transactional emails automatically. Never auto-unsubscribes — surfaces links for you to click.

⚡

Batch Processing

Groups senders intelligently and processes them in bulk. Handles hundreds of emails per category without breaking a sweat.

📊

Progress Tracking

Keeps a running tally — emails cleared, senders processed, money spent. Get a full summary when you're done.

How It Works

Three steps. That's it.

Say Less runs as a Claude Skill with Gmail integration. No API keys, no third-party services.

01

Connect Gmail

Enable the Gmail integration in Claude's settings under Integrations. Takes about 10 seconds.

02

Install the Skill

Add the Say Less skill to your Claude account. It includes both inbox cleanup and receipt scanning.

03

Just Talk

"Clean up my inbox", "scan my receipts from last month", "unsubscribe from everything". Say Less handles the rest.
